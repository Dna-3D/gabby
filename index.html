About Gabby's Collection

Born from the passion to provide stylish, affordable fashion to FUTO students. We believe that great style shouldn't break the bank.

Our Story

Gabby's Collection started as a small idea in a FUTO dormitory room. As students ourselves, we noticed the struggle to find trendy, quality clothing that fit our budget.

What began as sharing fashion finds with friends has grown into a trusted brand serving the entire FUTO community. We source the latest trends and negotiate the best prices to bring you fashion that speaks to your style.

Today, we're proud to be the go-to fashion destination for students who want to look great without compromising their meal money.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer campus delivery?

Yes! We provide free delivery within FUTO campus. Orders are typically delivered within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash on delivery for campus orders.

Can I return items if they don't fit?

Yes, we offer exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided items are in original condition.

Do you have a physical store?

We operate primarily online with campus delivery, but you can arrange to meet us on campus for fittings.

Are there student discounts available?

Our prices are already student-friendly! We also offer special discounts during exam periods and holidays.
